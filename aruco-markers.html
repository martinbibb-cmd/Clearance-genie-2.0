üìñ How to Use ArUco Markers

Step 1: Print Your Marker
  • Select a size above and print on matte paper
  • Ensure printing is at 100% scale (no resizing)
  • Cut along the outer edge, leaving the white border
Step 2: Position on Wall
  • Hold the marker flat against the wall you want to measure
  • Position it on the same plane as the equipment/flue
  • Ensure good lighting without reflections
Step 3: Calibrate with App
  • Open Clearance Genie ArUco calibration
  • Point camera at the marker until it's detected (green outline)
  • Wait for calibration to lock (5 consecutive detections)
  • Then capture your wall photo for measurement
