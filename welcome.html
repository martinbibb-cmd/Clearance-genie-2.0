How It Works

1 Prepare Your Calibration Card
Take a blue credit-card-sized plastic card and draw a thick black "X" from corner to corner on one side using a permanent marker. This will be your calibration reference.

üìã Calibration Card Instructions:

  • Use a blue, credit-card-sized plastic card
  • Draw a thick black "X" from corner to corner (5-8mm thick lines)
  • Keep the edges clean - don't round the card or add stickers
  • This gives the camera a clear, distinctive target to detect
2 Calibrate the Camera
Hold your blue card with the black X facing the camera, flat against the wall. The system will detect it and calculate accurate measurements.
3 Add Your Equipment
Manually enter the location and dimensions of your boilers, radiators, cylinders, and flues. The app uses simple forms - database integration coming later!
4 View Color-Coded Clearances
See instant visual feedback with color-coded clearance zones:
  • Flue: 100mm center with 150mm, 200mm, 300mm halos in different colors
  • Equipment: White boxes with clearance zones added
