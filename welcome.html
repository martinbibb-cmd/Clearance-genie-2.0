2 Calibrate with Camera

üìñ User Instructions

Before You Start:

  • Print your ArUco marker at 100% scale on matte paper
  • Ensure good lighting in the area you're measuring
  • Have a clear view of the wall and equipment

During Calibration:

  • Hold the marker flat against the wall surface
  • Position it on the same plane as the flue/equipment
  • Keep the marker visible until detection locks (green outline)
  • Wait for 5 consecutive detections for best accuracy

Taking Measurements:

  • After calibration, pull back to capture the full wall
  • Tap to mark the flue/equipment center first
  • Then tap to mark corners of windows, doors, and other obstacles
  • Review the color-coded clearance zones
  • Download the annotated photo for your records
